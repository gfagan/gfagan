Here's a little about me

I'm a UX and Functional Design Specialist with Accenture, working for a Major Health and Public Service client.

A former Business Information Systems graduate from UCC, I recently expanded on my love for all things related to User Experience and Interaction Design by completing a BSc Honours Degree in Digital Technology, Design and Innovation.

Since joining Accenture, I have overseen design, test and delivery of a major project, the redesign of an application, and most recently, an application review and roadplan delivery.

I have experience in a variety of areas, with a good basis across consulting, design, technology and development.

Prototyping

Research

Visual Design

Web Development

Functional Design

Test

Extensive experience in both Functional and User based testing through User Acceptance Testing, Usability Testing, User Interface Testing, and Integration Testing.
