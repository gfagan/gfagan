Scent - Start up Branding

Note: This section is still in progress

The Objective

To identify a potential start-up application idea and generate the necessary start-up branding content, such as a Logo and Brand Guidelines, as well as the key screens required to demo the core functionality.

Needfinding

I began by undertaking a needfinding process whereby I allowed myself a time period of around two weeks to look for any pain points that I experienced in daily living during that time, and from that, determined whether an application or product could be created to help resolve the matter hand.

One of the ideas that I contemplated during this time included a musical keyboard that would help user’s learn how to play songs by providing visual feedback to the user, highlighting what keys to press in tandem with the song of the user’s choice.

Another was an application that provided for a one-stop-shop when it came to booking and paying appointments, ranging from restaurants to hairdressers. This would also allow for the automatic splitting of bills between users.

Ultimately, I started to find that the ideas that excited me most were more in the line of applications that took advantage of the camera in your phone and allowed user’s to get instant feedback about an item or service upon scanning it with their phone. This was largely inspired by the fantastic application, Vivino, which provides users with wine ratings and reviews whenever a wine label, or wine menu, is scanned. I believe the potential in this type of service is endless, and could be used to provide feedback and ratings on anything from clothes to food

But the product that appealed to me the most, and I believe would be extremely beneficial to the market, is a perfume and cologne scanner. When wandering through the airport duty free and being greeted by endless rows of fragrances, I would love to be able to simply scan the label of one or many of the bottles and instantly get some information about a product, it’s community rating, user reviews, the average cost, the type of scent that can be expected, and ability to provide my own rating or review.
